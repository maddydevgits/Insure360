YSR Jagananna Saswatha Bhoomi

Eligibility Criteria: All farmers to provide ownership of their land in rural and urban areas

Benefits: Records residents' property ownership, provides proof card, and offers loans for rural and urban development.

YSR Donna Baddie Scheme

Eligibility Criteria: All farmers repaying the same within one year

Benefits: Interest subsidy provided to farmers availing crop loans.

Jagananna Herbs Lesotho Scheme

Eligibility Criteria: Financial stability for women

Benefits: Scheme aimed at providing women empowerment and social stability for SC, ST, BC.

Free Crop Insurance Scheme

Eligibility Criteria: Farmers across the state, free crop insurance scheme with physical verification of crops & easy enrollment process

Benefits: Protection of farmers against all excessive climate changes, natural calamities.

YSR Jalapeno Kala Scheme

Eligibility Criteria: Farmers can get benefits of free borewells drilling from the government, shortage of water and irrigation of 2.5 acres

Benefits: Utilize of groundwater available for small and marginal farmers.

Agriculture Electricity Cash Transfer Scheme

Eligibility Criteria: Free power supply provided to the agriculture sector. The bill amount will be credited to the bank accounts.

Benefits: Farming sector provides assistance in electricity bill payment and 2% limit 20,000 Crores as a loan.

Rythu Raksha Scheme

Eligibility Criteria: Small farmers, marginal and tenant farmers. This is all throughout Andhra Pradesh farmers only.

Benefits: Provide financial assistance to the farmers to prevent suicide. Rs. 13,500 per year.

YSR Ruthy Bharos-PM Kosam Scheme

Eligibility Criteria: This provides financial assistance to the farmers.

Benefits: Rs. 13,500 per year.
